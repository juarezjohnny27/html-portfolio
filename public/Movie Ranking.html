The Best Movies According to Johnny

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Gran Torino

One of Clint Eastwood's best films about him befriending an Asian neighbor who tried to steal his car.

Captain Phillips

Tom Hanks plays a captain of a ship that gets hijacked by Somali pirates

The Revenant

Leonardo DiCaprio plays the father of an Indian boy. Gets mauled by bear and tries to go save his son.
